Welcome to my personal website. This site serves as a blog, a repository of my work and perhaps as some sort of resumé. You can learn more about me, browse my own open-source projects or view my contributions to other open-source projects.

About me

I am a master student in Computer Science at the Delft University of Technology. I currently live in Rotterdam, The Netherlands. During the third year of my bachelor (Computer Science at the Delft University of Technology), I have studied in Stockholm, Sweden for six months at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology.

In the field of Computer Science, my interests are broad. Mainly, however, I am interested in operating systems, distributed systems and programming languages.

My greatest passion is open-source software, to which I strive to contribute in several different ways. I have been using Linux for over five years and participate in several Linux-related communities. This includes being a moderator at the Crunchbang Linux forum and the BunsenLabs Linux forum. When the Crunchbang Linux project ended, I played a role in forming the BunsenLabs community continuation.

I also maintain several open-source applications myself and contribute patches to other open-source projects. Also see my GitHub resumé.

If you wish to know more, please feel free to contact me via email.
